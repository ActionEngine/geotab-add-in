**Introduction**

Contains information related to a shipment.

**Properties**

## Account

The[ApiAccount](../ApiAccount/index.md)where the order was placed.

## ErpReference

Geotab shipment number.

## PurchaseOrderNo

Purchase order number for the associated order.

## ShipDate

Date of the shipment.

## TrackingNo

Tracking number from courier.

## TrackingURL

The URL to the courier's website where tracking information can be found.

## UserContact

The[ApiUserContact](../ApiUserContact/index.md)where shipment will be delivered.
