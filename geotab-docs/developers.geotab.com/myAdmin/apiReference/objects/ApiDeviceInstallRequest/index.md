**Introduction**

Class to track device lookups.

**Properties**

## Asset

The asset number of the device.

## Comments

Comments submitted at the time of installation.

## Device

The[ApiGeotabDevice](../ApiGeotabDevice/index.md)being requested.

## InstallerCompany

The name of the company that installed the device.

## InstallerName

The name of the installer who installed the device.

## Odometer

The odometer reading at the time of installation.

## RequestDateUtc

The date of the install log.
