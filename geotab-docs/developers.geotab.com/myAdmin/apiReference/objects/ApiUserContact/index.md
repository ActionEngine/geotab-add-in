**Introduction**

Contact information for MyAdmin user.

**Properties**

## Active

Indicates whether the contact is still active.

## Address

Contact's formatted address.

## City

The contact's city.

## ContactEmail

The contact's email address.

## Country

The contact's country.

## DisplayName

Contact's display name.

## FirstName

The contact's first name.

## Id

Unique ID of the record

## LastName

The contact's last name.

## ResellerCustomerId

An optional ID field that may be used by the reseller to identify the customer

## State

The contact's state or province.

## Street1

The first line of the contact's street address.

## Street2

The second line of the contact's street address.

## Telephone1

The contact's primary phone number.

## Telephone2

The contact's secondary phone number.

## UserCompany

The[ApiUserCompany](../ApiUserCompany/index.md)this contact belongs to.

## UserContactEmails

Optional bcc emails for the contact

## ZipCode

The contact's zip/postal code
