**Introduction**

Device contained in a[ApiRma](../ApiRma/index.md).

**Properties**

## Comments

Comments regarding the device.

## CompleteDateUtc

Date the device rma was completed.

## Device

The[ApiGeotabDevice](../ApiGeotabDevice/index.md)that belongs to the RMA.

## ExpireDateUtc

Date the device was expired.

## ProcessDateUtc

Date the device was processed.

## ProductDescription

Description of the product.

## Quantity

Quantity of the product.

## ReceivedBy

The[ApiUser](../ApiUser/index.md)who received the RMA.

## ReceivedDateUtc

Date the device was added to the RMA.

## ReturnInvoiceId

ID of the invoice.

## Rma

The[ApiRma](../ApiRma/index.md)that this record belongs to.

## ShipReference

Shipping reference code.

## WarrantyStatus

Indicates whether the device is under warranty.
