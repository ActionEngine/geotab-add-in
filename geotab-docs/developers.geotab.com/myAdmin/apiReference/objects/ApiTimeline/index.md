**Introduction**

Device timelines

**Properties**

## AssignmentDetails

Assignment details of the timeline.

## Category

Category of the timeline. (Request, Status, Response)

## Details

Details of the timeline.

## Device

The[ApiGeotabDevice](../ApiGeotabDevice/index.md)that belongs to the timeline.

## FromDate

Date and time of the timeline was started.

## Owner

Owner of the timeline.

## State

State of the timeline. (None, Single Event, Activation Event, Deactivation Event)

## Title

Title of the timeline.

## ToDate

Date and time of the timeline was ended; same as fromDate if it is not applicable to the timeline.

## User

User who created the timeline.
