**Introduction**

Pricing entry for a specific product

**Properties**

## BulkPrice

The bulk purchase price of the product. If non-zero, can be applied to quantities above the BulkPriceMinQuantity instead of the PurchasePrice.

## BulkPriceMinQuantity

The minimum quantity above which this product can be purchased for the bulk purchase price. The first BulkPriceMinQuantity products will be the normal PurchasePrice, additional quantities can be purchased at the Bulk Price.

## Currency

The currency of the prices

## LocalBulkPrice

The bulk purchase price in the user's local currency

## LocalPurchasePrice

The purchase price in the user's local currency

## MonthlyPrice

The monthly price of the product for approved resellers

## MsrpLocalPurchasePrice

The suggested retail price of the product in the user's local currency

## MsrpMonthlyPrice

The suggested retail price for the monthly cost of the product

## MsrpPurchasePrice

The suggested retail price of the product

## PricingType

The[ApiPricingType](../ApiPricingType/index.md)object for the pricing record

## ProductCode

The product code

## PurchasePrice

The purchase price of the product for approved resellers
