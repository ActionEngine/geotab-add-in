Engine shutdown record.

Engine shutdown in PC record.

## Exemption16H

## ExemptionOffDutyDeferral

Exemption off duty deferral.

Exemption reduced rest

## INT_D

Intermediate Drive Event.

## INT_PC

Intermediate Personal Conveyance Event.

User login record.

## Logoff

User logout record.

Missing data elements. Applies to[Malfunction](../../../../index.md#Malfunction)or[Diagnostic](../../../../index.md#Diagnostic).

Off-duty status.

## ON

On-duty status.

Special type of PC, will not be interrupted by automatic logs

ELD continually fails to acquire valid position measurement. Applies to[Malfunction](../../../../index.md#Malfunction).

Railroad Exemption

## Rest

Driver is not working in relation to a fatigue-regulated heavy vehicle who are not driving under BFM, AFM or exemption hours

## SB

When ELD date and time exceeds 10 minute offset from UTC. Applies to[Malfunction](../../../../index.md#Malfunction).

More than 30 minutes of driving with unidentified driving. Applies to[Diagnostic](../../../../index.md#Diagnostic).

## Work

Any tasks relating to the use of the vehicle

Work Exemption (permitted personal activity) refers to any work done by a driver for personal and non-commercial purposes

## WT

## YM

Yard move driver status.
