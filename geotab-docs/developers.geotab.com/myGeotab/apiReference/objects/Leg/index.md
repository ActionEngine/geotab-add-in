**Introduction**

A single leg of[Directions](../Directions/index.md)between origin and destination[Waypoint](../Waypoint/index.md)s.

**Properties**

## Destination

The destination[Waypoint](../Waypoint/index.md)of this leg.

## Distance

The distance estimate for this leg in kilometers.

## Duration

The travel time duration estimate for this leg.

## Origin

The origin[Waypoint](../Waypoint/index.md)of this leg.

## Sequence

The ordered sequence of this leg.

## Steps

The steps denoting information about each separate[Step](../Step/index.md)of the leg.
