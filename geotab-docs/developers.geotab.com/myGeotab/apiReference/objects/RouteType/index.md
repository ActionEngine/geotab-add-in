**Introduction**

A type of[Route](../Route/index.md).

**Properties**

## Basic

A basic route. Does not have a[Device](../Device/index.md)associated.

## Plan

A route with a plan of the time/distance it is expected to take from stop to stop. A plan route has a[Device](../Device/index.md)associated.

## Service

A route with a plan with completion criteria. A service route has a[Device](../Device/index.md)associated.
