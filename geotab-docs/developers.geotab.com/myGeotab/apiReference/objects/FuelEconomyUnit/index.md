**Introduction**

Various Fuel Economy units Geotab supports. Currently supported units: L/100 km, km/L, MPG (US), MPG (Imperial), km/gallon (US), and gal/100km.

**Properties**

## GallonPer100Km

The gallon per 100 km unit.

## KmPerGallon

The km per gallon (US) unit.

## KmPerLiter

The km per liter unit.

## LitersPer100Km

The liters per 100 km unit.

## MPGImperial

The miles per gallon (Imperial).

## MPGUS

The miles per gallon (US).
