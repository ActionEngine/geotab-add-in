**Introduction**

A collection of properties based on[FuelTaxDetail](../FuelTaxDetail/index.md)elements. Used to populate a row in a fuel tax report.

**Properties**

## Authority

The authority.

## Country

The country.

## Device

The device.

## Distance

The distance based on the odometer values at the begin and end times.

## Driver

The driver.

## EnterGpsOdometer

The GPS odometer value at the begin time.

## EnterOdometer

The odometer value at the begin time.

## EnterTime

The begin time.

## ExitGpsOdometer

The GPS odometer value at the end time`.

## ExitOdometer

The odometer value at the end time.

## ExitTime

The end time.

## FuelEconomy

The fuel economy.

## FuelPurchased

The purchased fuel volume.

## FuelPurchasedByType

The purchased fuel volume by fuel type.

## FuelType

The fuel type.

## FuelUsed

The used fuel volume.

## GpsDistance

The distance based on Earth distance between GPS records.

## IsIftaJurisdiction

A value indicating whether the jurisdiction is an IFTA jurisdiction.

## Jurisdiction

The jurisdiction.

## Latitude

The latitude at the end time.

## LicensePlate

The license plate.

## Longitude

The longitude at the end time.

## NetTaxableFuel

The net taxable fuel volume.

## OdometerOffset

The current odometer offset for the[Device](../Device/index.md).

## RoadType

The road type.

## TollRoad

The toll road.

## UpdatesAvailable

A value indicating whether there are updates to this fuel tax detail.

## Vin

The VIN.
